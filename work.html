TU ESTILO

EDITORIAL MAGAZINE

THE BRIEF

More and more we are turning to be a paperless society and with that we are changing the way we used to do simple things such as reading an actual newspaper or buying a print magazine. We have changed many things to accommodate our daily routines and lifestyle. Now finding information and getting inspired it's just a click away. For our 4th project on Ironhack we were asked to work on a e-Magazine of our choice with the guidance of the profile of the user persona we decide to choose.

THE GOAL

My goal was to design a responsive mobile website for a fashion and article magazine inspired with the hispanic culture and trends.

USER PERSONA

The accumulation of the different insights and common patterns that came from the users answers helped me create the user persona.

COMPETITORS RESEARCH

Competitive feature analysis tool helped me to understand where TU ESTILO blue ocean market position was, It wasn’t so hard to recognize where the e-magazine position was. However in order to be positioned in the blue ocean it would required to combine fashion for more realistic woman and inspiring stories.

I searched and compared the websites and discovered that most of them have the same type of models and stereotypes.The competitors I explored were Vogue and Cosmopolitan.

LO-FIDELITY WIREFRAMES

Lo-Fidelity wireframes were tested amoung the users with a 100% direct success.
However, during user testing I found that within the tested users there were 16.7% of mis clicks.
After the testing of the lo fidelity prototypes I then proceeded to create the mid fidelity wireframes.

MID-FIDELITY

For the mid-fidelity prototype the main goal for our users was to locate the inspiring stories, find an article about a hispanic woman helping other girls from the comumnity, then click save to save the article for later.

MOODBOARD

While conducting the desirability testing I notice that I was pretty much on point and the users agreed that the images I used were transmitting a sense of fresh, fun, feminine, confident and trendy.
